Article 2

Link to article 2: Predicting food demand in food courts by decision tree approaches

This week I found another article related to my research. The paper is about predicting food demand in food courts by decision tree approaches. From an economic point of view, preparing the wrong amount of food creates economic problems. If the supply exceeds the demand it leads to food waste and companies that make food lose their money and resources. On the other hand, if the demand is higher than the supply of food, the restaurant may lose their customer and prestige. Having a computer program that can predict the amount of food can benefit food suppliers in many ways. The research uses data mining and tree decision methods to predict the demand for food. Data mining is discovering the relationship, correlations, and trends in data with the help of algorithms. Data mining consists of various techniques such as neural networks, statistics, decision trees, and genetic algorithms. In the research, to study food demand the researchers collected the input data such as seasonal demand change, holidays along with the number of food consumed each day. Three different decision tree algorithms (CART, CHAID, and Mircosoft Decision Trees) are implemented to predict menu-based food consumption in a university food court. The decision tree is a data mining technique usually used for classification and prediction. The authors choose a decision tree in their research because it is an easily understandable and interpretable model, quick to build, and adaptable to data change. Moreover, decision tree methods also have high precision accuracy, so it is a trustable choice for this project. The main two tools they used in this project are SPSS Clementine and Microsoft Analysis Service.

Link to article 3: TBayesian approach for predicting food and beverage sales in staff canteens and restaurants

The research focuses on a forecasting approach that is based on the data from the point-of-sale systems and allows for a straightforward human interpretation. The research aimed to predict the daily sold of the item in a menu based restaurant. Accurate demand forecasting is crucial for success in restaurant and dinning hall management. For the environment aspect, predicting food demand helps reduce food waste and contribute greatly to sustainable development. Moreover, having a correct amount of food to prepare will increase the profit of the restaurants. For the method, the authors used two Bayesian generalized additive models (GAMs) for predicting demand. The first model assumes a normally distributed target and the second assumes the response follows a negative binomial distribution. The outcome of the research is that both models provide a reasonable fit for the time series data. My intended project is about predicting food demand in a restaurant, it is the same idea with this article. This research used the data from a restaurant point of sales system. In my future research, I may include more data such as weather forecast, seasons and also weekdays in the data to generate the food demand prediction.

Article 4

Link to article 4: Food Demand Prediction Using the Nonlinear Autoregressive Exogenous Neural Network

The second article talks about food demand prediction using the nonlinear autoregressive exogenous neutral network. This is the link to the article: article 2. Food demand prediction is an important issue to both business and sustainable development. Predicting accurate food demand can reduce the business operation cost by reducing inventory fee and increasing customer satisfaction. For the environment issue, food waste and loss have been the topic draw much attention in recent years. Data shows that 1.3 tons of food is waste each year, approximately one third of the food produced. In the supply food chain, the food waste in the consumption phase is the largest about 40% to 60%. Modern food system should be designed to prevent overproduction, over-abundance and waste. Compared to the qualitative prediction method, the quantitative method involving data and statistic method has a better performance. There are different predicting models have been used before, this research used the nonlinear autoregressive exogenous neural network (NARXNN) for food demand prediction. In this research, the authors train the neural network topologies and then simulated it for demand prediction. Therefore, two dataset were used: one for training process and one for testing the prediction. The result of the study is that NARX model based on artificial intelligence can create highly accurate food demand forecast. The nonlinear autoregressive exogenous and neural network is an effective method for time series predicting model. This research is closely related to what I wanted to do. In the future, I want to study more about the method they use in this research and apply it to my senior project.
